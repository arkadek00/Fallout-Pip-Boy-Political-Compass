Calibration notes

Comparison is relative to the Fallout wasteland, not a modern-state baseline. Scarcity, militarization, and post-collapse conditions push even benevolent factions closer to hierarchy than many real-world analogues.

Game-specific splits remain central: Fallout 3 Brotherhood is notably softer than the New Vegas or Fallout 4 chapters; NCR in New Vegas drifts more imperial and managerial than its Fallout 2 form.
